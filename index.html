My Vision

The Billionaire in Progress Project is not just about me becoming a billionaire—it’s about providing a roadmap for others to achieve extraordinary wealth in Godly, practical ways.

I am using myself as a guinea pig because I believe if someone like me, starting from zero, can achieve massive wealth, others can too.

My Goals

  • Become a millionaire ($1,000,000) by August 12, 2030 (5 years).
  • Become a billionaire ($1,000,000,000) by August 12, 2035 (10 years).

Milestones on My Journey

Here’s a clear roadmap showing the key milestones in my “Billionaire in Progress” project:

  1. 2025: Starting point – $0 in my account. Commit to learning, skill-building, and mindset development.
  2. 2026–2029: Building foundation – Saving, investing, and applying high-value skills. Track small wins and lessons.
  3. 2030: First major milestone – Become a millionaire ($1,000,000). Refine strategies for exponential growth.
  4. 2031–2034: Scaling phase – Leverage technology, high-impact entrepreneurship, and God-centered strategies to multiply wealth.
  5. 2035: Ultimate milestone – Achieve billionaire status ($1,000,000,000) while inspiring others and providing a roadmap for Godly, practical wealth creation.

Each milestone is a checkpoint for learning, applying strategies, and inspiring others who are willing to commit to building wealth in purposeful ways.

How I Will Achieve This

1. Becoming a Millionaire (2025–2030)

  • Invest strategically each year, adjusting based on returns.
  • Explore high-growth opportunities and allow money to compound.
  • Continuously educate myself on finance, wealth creation, and entrepreneurship.
  • Invest $120,000–$200,000 per year if needed, leveraging discipline, creative ventures, and skills.

2. Becoming a Billionaire (2030–2035)

  • Pursue scalable entrepreneurship opportunities and innovative businesses.
  • Solve big problems creating massive value for millions.
  • Take calculated, strategic risks guided by faith and informed decision-making.
  • Continuously grow knowledge, network, influence, and impact.

What Gives Me the Audacity to Believe I Can Do This

  • Valuing My Time: I value my time at $50,000 per hour, focusing only on high-impact, high-value activities.
  • Radical Faith: Deuteronomy 8:18 says God gives us the power to get wealth. God provides me with ability, strategy, and influence to create, control, and multiply resources.
  • Cultivated Mindset: Over a decade of studying millionaire and billionaire mindsets. Now it’s time to put knowledge into action.
  • Skills and Expertise: Sales, marketing, and tech skills combined to create scalable ventures.

My Commitment

I will act with discipline, creativity, and faith. I will invest in myself, my skills, and opportunities aligned with my vision. I will take consistent action every day toward building wealth and impact.

For Others

If you are willing to put in the work, cultivate the right mindset, and commit to disciplined, faith-driven action, this project will provide insights, strategies, and lessons to help you achieve your own financial breakthroughs.

Starting from nothing is not a barrier. Faith, strategy, and effort can create massive impact. Kingdom wealth principles combined with modern strategies can transform lives.
